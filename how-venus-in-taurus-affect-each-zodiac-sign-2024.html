Venus in Taurus 2024 Will Affect Every Zodiac Sign

Slip into your bathrobe, put on a face mask, and bask in some well-deserved self-care. In addition to being in a Venus-ruled season, the love planet will soon join the sun in its sign of rulership, Taurus. If you’re wondering how Venus in Taurus will affect each zodiac sign, you’re in for a treat as this sensually driven transit invites you to slow down, so you can stop and smell the roses. 

Venus is the planet of love, beauty, and pleasure. It governs our romantic relationships, our preferred aesthetic, and our definition of harmony. The zodiac sign it transits— considering its archetype and celestial persona—influences how these qualities manifest in our lives and in the collective. When it comes to love, Venus often plays the role of a celestial matchmaker, guiding us toward romantic experiences that align with its journey through the zodiac. The love planet not only shapes our external relationships but also your values, your self-worth, and the way you perceive yourself.

After an adrenaline-inducing journey through Aries—where the love planet stoked passions and ignited new beginnings—Venus sets the mood for a rich and lush journey through Taurus. If something (or someone) set your heart ablaze with passion during Venus in Aries, you will love what comes next. The love planet makes its debut in Taurus on April 29 at 7:31 a.m. ET, bringing your manifestations down to earth and anchoring your intentions so they blossom into something fruitful and long-lasting. Your romantic relationships are no exception. 

On May 1, Venus will square Pluto in Aquarius, bringing a surge of intensity and transformation regarding matters of love, finances, and stability. Tensions are bound to rise, so be sure to stay grounded as deep-seated emotions resurface. More importantly, steer away from power struggles. On May 13, Venus will join Saturn in a harmonious sextile, providing us with the opportunity to consolidate and solidify financial and/or relationship matters.

The love planet will eventually join forces with Uranus, revolutionizing our values while adding an electric backdrop to our love lives. Uranus is the planet of innovation, independence, and sudden changes, bringing a galvanizing rush to your relationships, your style choices, and your financial status. Before concluding its journey through this fixed earth sign, Venus will join forces with Jupiter in Taurus and sextile Neptune in Pisces on May 23. In addition to enhancing our capacity for love and compassion, the influence of Jupiter and Neptune inspires a deeper level of intimacy and fulfillment.
